Store Search

Making the finding better and faster when Store Locators allows customers to search by different criteria.

  • Search by distance (within the specified radius from the current location).
  • Search by area (within a specific state/ province, country, city, zip code)
  • Filter by tags (product types, product brands,...)

Street/Direction view

The search results of stores which meet all conditions will be shown on the Store List page and especially, the clear direction is always ready to give your customers the fastest way to get the needed place. Along with detailed directions on map/satellite mode, they even can explore any store locations through 360-degree, panoramic, and street-level imagery.

Magento Store Locator Contact

In order to be more friendly with your customers, a lot of store information is displayed including Address, Phone, Email, Opening & Closing hours, Store Locator on Google Map and some of the store images. Even they can approach to any review of store’s clients via Facebook if you accept. This function is the awesome solution to create the huge reliability for your customers who only reach you on the internet in the first time.

Google Map Integration

By installing Store Locator module, Google Map is already integrated. Each chosen store will be appeared instantly enclosing to store icon for that.

Unlimited new store locators

Magento Store Locator allows store owners to enable or disable any store which belongs to your chain of store quickly as well as provide all details of store and specially to the searchers, set up opening & closing time separately on normal days and holidays.

Time schedule

Custom interface templates

Support for SEO
